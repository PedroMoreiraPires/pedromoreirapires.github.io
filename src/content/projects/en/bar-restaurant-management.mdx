# Internal Management for Bars and Restaurants

An all-in-one solution for the efficient administration of dining establishments. From stock control to employee shift management, this application centralizes all critical operations.

## System Modules

- **Real-Time Inventory**: Detailed control of ingredients and beverages, with low stock alerts.
- **Order Management**: System for taking orders from tables or the bar, sending them directly to the kitchen.
- **Staff Management**: Management of schedules, shifts, payroll, and employee performance.
- **Admin Panel**: Sales statistics, most popular dishes, and financial performance.

## Technologies

- **Backend**: Java with Spring Boot for secure and scalable business logic.
- **Frontend**: React for an agile and interactive user experience.
- **Database**: MongoDB to handle flexible data such as menus and order configurations.

Designed to optimize time and reduce losses, this tool is essential for any bar or restaurant manager looking to modernize their management.
