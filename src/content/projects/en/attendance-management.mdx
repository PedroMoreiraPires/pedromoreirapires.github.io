# Attendance Management

An integral system for controlling attendance and physical presence, designed for companies and event organizers who require precise tracking of participation.

## Features

- **Check-in/Check-out Registration**: Clocking methods via QR code, geolocation, or biometrics.
- **Automatic Reports**: Generation of daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports exportable to Excel and PDF.
- **Notifications**: Automatic alerts for absences, late arrivals, or inconsistencies in the records.
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: Visualization of the current attendance status of the workforce or event attendees.

## Technology Stack

- **Java**: Core of the business logic.
- **Docker**: Application containerization to facilitate deployment and scalability.
- **SQL**: Relational database to guarantee the integrity of attendance records.

The application focuses on reliability and ease of use, allowing administrators to manage large volumes of attendance data without complications.
